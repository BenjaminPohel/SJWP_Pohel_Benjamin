Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3 cup warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 cup gramulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tabelspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup butter

Insturctions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the floor and mix untril combined into a dough
